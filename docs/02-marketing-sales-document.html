4Benefits (Not Just Features)

Sales tip Contractors buy outcomes, not software. Lead with the benefit ("get paid faster"), then explain the feature ("online invoice payment via Stripe"). Never lead with the technology.
💵

Get Paid Faster

Online payment via Stripe means customers can pay by card or bank transfer the moment they receive the invoice. Average contractors wait 45–60 days for payment. Clear Build customers get paid in days, not weeks. One online payment per month more than covers the $49 subscription.

🏆

Look More Professional Than Your Competitors

Branded quotes and invoices with your logo, professional layout, and online approval make your business look established and trustworthy. Customers choose contractors they trust - and a professionally delivered quote builds that trust before you even meet.

⏱️

Save 5–10 Hours Per Week on Admin

Auto-numbered documents, quote-to-invoice conversion, payment reminders, and email templates eliminate repetitive admin. What used to take an hour now takes minutes. That time goes back to billable work or to going home earlier.

🔒

Protect Yourself from Scope Disputes

Digital change orders with customer approval create a paper trail for every scope change. No more "you said you'd include that" disputes. Every addition is documented, priced, and approved before work starts.

📈

Win More Jobs with Better Lead Follow-Up

The lead pipeline and heat scores surface which prospects need attention today. Studies show 80% of sales require 5+ follow-ups, yet 44% of contractors give up after one. Clear Build keeps follow-ups visible so no opportunity falls through the cracks.

🗂️

Everything in One Place - Cancel Your Other Subscriptions

The average contractor uses 4–7 separate apps (calendar, invoicing, CRM, notes, messaging). Clear Build replaces most of them. One login, one monthly payment, all your business data connected and searchable.

👷

Scale Your Team Without Losing Control

Role-based access means your crew sees their jobs, your admins see financials, and you stay in control. Add and remove team members instantly. No shared passwords, no data leaks, no confusion about who can do what.

Competitive Objection Handling

ObjectionResponse
"I already use Jobber."Jobber's core scheduling is great but their CRM is basic, change orders are limited, and they charge $149/mo for features we include at $49/mo. Many contractors run both for a month then switch.
"I use FreshBooks for invoicing."FreshBooks is excellent for freelancers. It doesn't have lead tracking, change orders, project management, or scheduling. Clear Build is built for the construction lifecycle, not generic services.
"ServiceTitan is the industry standard."ServiceTitan is built for large field service companies ($400+/month, sales calls required, implementation takes weeks). Clear Build is specifically for contractors under 50 people who need to be running today, not after a 30-day onboarding.

7Launch Timeline & Marketing Calendar

Week 1–2 · June 2026

Soft Launch - Founder & Friends

Invite 20–30 contractors personally known to the founding team. Offer 3 months free in exchange for feedback and testimonials. Set up support channel. Fix any critical bugs found in real usage.

Week 3–4 · June 2026

Public Beta - Organic Traffic

Publish landing page to clearbuildusa.com. Submit to Product Hunt. Post in 5 construction Facebook groups (200K+ combined members). Activate Google Business profile. Publish first 3 blog posts for SEO.

Month 2 · July 2026

Paid Acquisition Start

Launch Google Ads targeting "contractor invoicing software", "construction estimate app", "contractor management app". Target $500–$1,000/month ad spend initially. A/B test 3 landing page variants. Start retargeting trial users who didn't convert.

Month 3 · August 2026

Content & Referral Engine

Launch referral programme ($25 credit per referred paid user). Publish 10 SEO articles. Reach out to 10 YouTube contractor channels for reviews/sponsorships. Submit to G2, Capterra, Trustpilot for reviews. Target: 25 reviews by end of month.

Month 4–6 · Sep–Nov 2026

Partnership Channel

Approach NAHB local chapters for partnership. Reach out to 5 construction-focused accountants for referral partnerships ($100/converted referral). Co-market with a building materials supplier for co-branded flyers at point-of-sale.

Q1 2027

Scale

Increase paid ads to $5K+/month (if CAC < $300). Hire first sales/support person. Launch affiliate programme. Target 500 paying subscribers.
